Resources

It's important for any designer to take advantage of the many outlets that you can find online. Here are some of my favorite sites.

Adobe Kuler

This is a great site for experimenting with color palettes. It offers preset palettes, community created ones, and you can even make your own based off of pictures.

Font Squirrel

This site offers some of the best free fonts for download. All of the fonts here are very useful and have good quality.

Tumblr

A very important aspect in design is having getting inspiration from other designers. There are a number of great blogs on tumblr that I have looked at when I'm behind deadline and need some ideas.
